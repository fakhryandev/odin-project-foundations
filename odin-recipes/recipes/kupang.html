Lontong Kupang

Lontong kupang or kupang lontong is the name of a food typical of the East Java region. This food is famous especially in the Arekan area of East Java

Ingredients

Kupang sauce Ingredients:

  • 300 grams of peeled kupang
  • 5 shrimps
  • 1 liter of water
  • 4 cloves of garlic, thinly sliced
  • 5 stalks of green onions
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon broth powder

Fine Seasoning Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of Sidoarjo mussel paste
  • 1 raw garlic clove
  • 2-3 cayenne pepper
  • lime slices (just take the juice)

Cook Instructions

  1. Boil water, add whole shrimps that have been washed. Cook for about 20 minutes to make the broth.
  2. Just strain the broth so it's not too cloudy.
  3. Thinly slice the garlic and chives. Saut√© until cooked, put into the broth, stir well.
  4. Enter the mussels, cook until boiling, add salt, sugar and broth powder to taste. The taste is a bit bland but more towards sweet, not salty.
  5. For serving, grind the spices, transfer to a plate. (If the Kupang Lontong seller, pulverize it directly on a serving plate). The taste tends to be salty and savory, and the taste of shrimp paste is generally different from shrimp paste.
  6. Cut the lontong into pieces, add the kneaded lentho and then pour over the hot Kupang sauce.
