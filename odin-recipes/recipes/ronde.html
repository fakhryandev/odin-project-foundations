Ronde

Ronde is a traditional Chinese food with the original name Tāngyuán.

Ingredients

Ronde Ball Material:

  • 100 grams of glutinous rice flour
  • 25 grams of sago flour
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 100 ml of hot water
  • Food coloring to taste

Stuffing :

  • 50 grams of peanuts, peeled, roasted, puree
  • 50 grams of brown sugar / granulated sugar, according to taste

Ginger drink :

  • 250 ml of water
  • 50 grams of ginger according to taste
  • 30 grams of brown sugar according to taste
  • 30 grams of sugar according to taste
  • 1 stalk of lemongrass

Cooking Instructions

  1. Mix sago flour and glutinous rice flour. Add hot water little by little while kneading until smooth.
  2. Prepare the stuffing by mixing the roasted peanuts with brown sugar. Mix well.
  3. Take a teaspoon of ball dough then fill it, shape it into a round shape resembling a ball. Do it until the dough runs out.
  4. Boil water, add ginger, lemon grass and sugar. Enter the round ball, cook until it floats.
