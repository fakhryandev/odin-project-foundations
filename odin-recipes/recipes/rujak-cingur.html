Rujak Cingur

Rujak cingur is one of the traditional foods that is easy to find in East Java, especially in its hometown of Surabaya.

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 250 gr beef mouth meat.
  • 100 grams of thinly sliced pineapple
  • 100 grams yam sliced thin.
  • 2 thinly sliced ​cucumbers
  • 1 young mango thinly sliced
  • 1 piece of tempeh, which has been fried and cut into cubes
  • 2 pieces of tofu, which has been fried and diced.
  • 100 grams of kale that has been boiled.
  • 50 gr of bean sprouts, brewed with hot water.
  • 2 pieces of lontong, cut into pieces.

Paste sauce ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of tamarind water
  • 200 ml of boiled water.
  • 50 grams of fried peanuts
  • 2 cloves of garlic.
  • 1 piece of banana
  • 10 grams of brown sugar.
  • 1 teaspoon of shrimp paste.
  • 2 tablespoons of shrimp paste
  • 1 teaspoon of salt.

Cook Instructions

  1. The first step is to boil the cingur or beef tongue until cooked and tender, then cut into pieces.
  2. Then make the petis sauce by finely mashing all the ingredients for the sauce and adding enough tamarind and water and stirring until thickened.
  3. Then arrange the pieces of rice cake, kale, and sliced ​​fruit and bean sprouts on a serving plate.
  4. Then flush with petis sauce then serve with starch crackers.
